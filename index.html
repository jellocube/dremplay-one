Dremplay One

Version 0.0.1.6.5.2 · Infinite Detail Voxel Engine

A portable first-person microvoxel engine built around mathematical Resources, voxel octaves, tagged volume formats, and predictive multiresolution streaming. The stable world currently renders through four octave bands while the new conversion architecture is introduced incrementally. 768×416×768 resident grid = 76.8 m × 41.6 m × 76.8 m at true 0.1 m isotropic resolution. A procedural far-field continues the terrain and water horizon as far as 1 km without allocating a cubic kilometer of editable voxels. Raymarched per-pixel, no polygons. Walkable ramps & stairs, smooth lighting + AO, fine carving & building. LMB/RMB to edit.

Test zone cancels the full load and builds a centered quarter-size world for rapid testing.

Help

Desktop controls

WASD move · Shift jump · Ctrl crawl · Space toggles cursor/game control · Mouse look

LMB carves · RMB places the selected material · / opens the console

Device modes

Dremplay Navigator is the dedicated phone interface with the upper two-thirds drag viewport and lower thumb controls. Tablet Touch keeps touch movement and actions with the robust window layout. PC Desktop retains mouse, keyboard, pointer lock, and the complete editor layout.

Touch controls

Navigator keeps the full-height world visible. Drag anywhere in the upper two-thirds to look. The divider marks the lower thumb area without blocking the world behind it. The cross D-pad responds continuously in all eight directions; A/B place and carve, with Jump and Crawl above and below them. Select opens every tool and Start opens the map.

Window hotkeys

/ Console · M Materials · R Resources · G Map · L Loading Monitor · F10 Server · F12 Options · F11 Mods · F1 Help · F2 About

Map and fast travel

Open the heightmap with G or the mobile Start button. The gold cross marks your position; tap land to move the observer immediately. Coarse mathematical terrain appears first and disposable fine voxels refine afterward.

Pointer control

Space toggles cursor control. Escape goes back or cancels; when leaving game control it highlights the first menu item.

AI Loading Monitor

The monitor classifies active work, estimates progress rate, and exposes statistical, predictive, simulation, Resource, weather, and GPU operations. Its close button always works and it stays closed while background work continues. Open it manually with L, the top menu, or Select → Loading Monitor. Navigator omits the conveyor for a quiet, efficient phone display; Tablet and Desktop use the continuous compact belt. Drag the title bar to move it or use the square button to minimize and restore it.

Windows

Use the top menu, Navigator Select launcher, or displayed key to open tools. Navigator windows fill the usable phone screen and provide large close controls; Tablet and Desktop retain robust movable layouts.
