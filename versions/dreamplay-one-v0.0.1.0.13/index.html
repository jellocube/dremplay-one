Dreamplay One

Version 0.0.1.0.13

A first-person microvoxel engine with true 0.1 m isotropic detail. Chunked streaming + natural-systems terrain. 768×416×768 resident grid = 76.8 m × 41.6 m × 76.8 m at true 0.1 m isotropic resolution. Raymarched per-pixel, no polygons. Walkable ramps & stairs, smooth lighting + AO, fine carving & building. LMB/RMB to edit.

Test zone cancels the full load and builds a centered quarter-size world for rapid testing.
